WHY WHAT YOU EAT MATTERS


In Ghana and across the world, health begins with what’s on your plate. A balanced diet, one that includes the right mix of proteins, vegetables, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals, isn’t just about eating enough; it’s about eating right. It supports growth in children, gives energy to workers, helps mothers care for families, and protects all of us from preventable illnesses like high blood pressure, diabetes, and malnutrition.

THE ROLE OF FARMING IN NATIONAL HEALTH


Farming isn’t just an occupation,it’s a responsibility. The Ghana Food-Based Dietary Guidelines emphasize the need to eat a variety of safe, nutritious foods every day, especially animal-source proteins like chicken and eggs, which are vital for strength and development. But in today’s fast-moving world, many families struggle to access food that is both healthy and convenient.

THAT'S WHERE WE COME IN
